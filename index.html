About DMS Tripura

The Disaster Management System (DMS) of Tripura is dedicated to providing real-time disaster tracking, emergency response, and resource management during critical situations.

Real-time Tracking

Monitor disasters and emergency situations as they develop.

Resource Management

Find and reserve emergency resources including shelters, medical facilities, and supplies.

Community Reports

Enable community participation in disaster reporting and response.
